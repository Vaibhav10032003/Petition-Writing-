Features

  • You can put up any grievances which you genuinly think should be addressed by the administration.
  • If the number of votes on post increases a certain limit, the authorities will come to know what are the grievances of the students.
  • If the community finds some post to be irrelevant then, they can downvote a partiular post, and if the votes fall below a certin limit, the post will be deleted.

Instructions

  • Please try to keep your post Respectful, as it is a community page
  • Please try to keep your post compact and impactful.
  • Please mention your name, branch and year at the end of your post.
  • Please do not put up your personal grievances here, look out for the matter that is genuine and affects a large number of students.
  • In case of any querry regarding the page or any feedback, feel free to reach out to me!! :)
